## Restaurant Options — Old Town Scottsdale & Nearby

- Brunch-focused but open for dinner
- Big, lively atmosphere — kids won't feel out of place
- Large party friendly
- Location: Scottsdale Quarter area

- Modern Latin cuisine
- Beautiful Old Town patio

## Tips for Dining with 14

- **Call every restaurant in advance.** Most places need notice for 14. Some will require a deposit or set a time window.
- **Consider splitting into 2 tables of 7** if a restaurant can't do one long table. Still eat together, just at adjacent tables.
- **Have a backup.** If the first choice can't accommodate, know your Plan B.
- **Ask about kids' menus** when you call — most of these places have them.
- **Tip well.** Large parties are hard to serve. 20%+ is the move, especially if gratuity isn't auto-added.
- **Lunch can be informal.** Save the reservations for dinner. Lunch can be grab-and-go, hotel-area spots, or zoo food on Zoo Day.
