## Day 2 — Zoo + Papago Park Day

### Morning
| Time | Activity | Who | Location |
|------|----------|-----|----------|
| 7:30 AM | Breakfast | Everyone | Hotel or nearby café |
| 9:00 AM | Phoenix Zoo — arrive at open | Everyone (14) | Phoenix Zoo (~10 min drive) |
| 9:00–12:00 PM | Zoo exploration | Everyone | Phoenix Zoo |

> Sub-groups can form naturally inside the zoo. Kids will set the pace. Adults can rotate between kid duty and exploring on their own.

### Midday
| Time | Activity | Who | Location |
|------|----------|-----|----------|
| 12:00–1:00 PM | Lunch | Everyone | Zoo café or pack lunches |
| 1:00–2:30 PM | Papago Park easy hike | Interested group | Papago Park (~5 min from Zoo) |
| 1:00–2:30 PM | Head back to hotel / pool | Others | Hilton |

### Afternoon
| Time | Activity | Who | Location |
|------|----------|-----|----------|
| 3:00–5:30 PM | Pool time & regroup | Everyone | Hilton pool |

### Evening
| Time | Activity | Who | Location |
|------|----------|-----|----------|
| 6:30 PM | Group dinner | Everyone (14) | TBD — see Dining Guide |
| 8:00 PM+ | Games, walks, hang out | Flexible | Hotel / Old Town |

**Day 2 Notes:**
- Zoo + Papago is the big group outing. Commit to the morning and let the afternoon breathe.
- Papago Park is right next to the Zoo — easy combo for those with energy left.
- Bring sunscreen, hats, water, and a stroller if needed for the kids.
