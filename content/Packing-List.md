# Scottsdale Family Vacation – Packing List

> Tailored for Arizona desert climate, pool days, hiking, and group dining.

---

## Everyone

### Sun & Heat Essentials
- [ ] Sunscreen SPF 50+ (bring extra — you'll go through it)
- [ ] Lip balm with SPF
- [ ] Sunglasses
- [ ] Hat (wide brim or baseball cap)
- [ ] Reusable water bottle (at least 1 per person)

### Clothing
- [ ] Shorts / light pants (daily wear)
- [ ] T-shirts / tank tops
- [ ] Light layers for evening (desert cools down at night)
- [ ] Comfortable walking shoes
- [ ] Sandals / flip-flops (pool and casual)
- [ ] Swimsuit(s) — at least 2 recommended for daily pool use
- [ ] Cover-up or lightweight shirt for pool-to-restaurant transitions

### Toiletries
- [ ] Toothbrush & toothpaste
- [ ] Deodorant
- [ ] Shampoo / conditioner (or rely on hotel)
- [ ] Any personal medications
- [ ] Aloe vera gel (for sunburn, just in case)
- [ ] Moisturizer (desert air is dry)

### Tech & Travel
- [ ] Phone charger
- [ ] Portable battery pack
- [ ] Headphones
- [ ] ID / license
- [ ] Insurance card
- [ ] Cash (some spots may be cash-only)

---

---

## Arizona-Specific Reminders

- **Hydrate constantly.** The dry heat sneaks up on you. Drink water even when you don't feel thirsty.
- **Mornings are golden.** Outdoor activities should skew early. By midday, shade and pool are your friends.
- **Evenings cool off.** A light jacket or long-sleeve layer is useful once the sun drops.
- **Skin care matters.** Between sun, chlorine, and dry air, moisturizer and aloe are worth packing.
