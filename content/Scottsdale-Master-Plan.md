## Activities – Master List

### Hiking

**Papago Park**

* Easy, scenic desert hiking
* Suitable for adults and kids
* Short trails, iconic red rock formations
* Ideal to pair with Zoo day or a lighter morning

**Tom's Thumb Trail (McDowell Sonoran Preserve)**

* Challenging, adults-only hike
* Steep elevation gain, high-reward views
* Best early morning with water and heat planning

---

### Phoenix Zoo

* Full-group anchor activity
* Kid-friendly with plenty for adults
* Best as a dedicated morning outing
* Allows natural sub-groups inside the zoo

---

### Swimming

* Located at Hilton Scottsdale Village Mirage pool
* Daily midday or afternoon anchor activity
* High participation for both adults and kids
* Easy regroup point between excursions

---

### Solidcore

* Adult-only fitness option
* Requires advance class booking
* Best early morning or late afternoon
* Works well as a split activity while others rest or swim

---

## Natural Trip Flow (Suggested)

* **Morning options:**

  * Tom's Thumb hike (adults)
  * Solidcore class (adults)
  * Easy morning / breakfast / pool for others

* **Midday anchor:**

  * Pool time at Hilton
  * Lunch and regroup

* **Dedicated group outing:**

  * Phoenix Zoo + Papago Park combo

* **Afternoons:**

  * Bookstore visits
  * Pool, naps, downtime

* **Evenings:**

  * Group dinners
  * Casual hangs, walks, games

---

## Notes

* Hilton serves as the primary regroup point each day
* Activities naturally support splitting into sub-groups without losing cohesion
* Kids have consistent swim + Zoo options, adults have fitness and hiking challenges
